Our Process

  • Project Briefing Establish a Vision
    Based on the details of your project, budget and timeline, our designer will provide you with their vision and ideas on how to beautifully prepare the property.
  • Consultation Details, Details
    Our team will meet you at the property to discuss details, address the condition, provide recommendations as required.
  • Preparation The Secret Sauce
    Our team will take away all the information and use their years of professional experience to build floor plans, hand pick all the furniture down to the smallest accent to be used to set-up your property.
  • Staging (Set-Up) The Wow! Day
    Our team will transform your listing to a buyer's dream. We will set-up everything for you and have it ready for the photo-shoot.
