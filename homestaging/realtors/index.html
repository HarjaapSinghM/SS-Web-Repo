Our Process

  • Project Briefing Establish a Vision
    Based on the details of your project, budget and timeline, our designer will provide you with their vision and ideas on how to beautifully prepare your listing.
  • Consultation Details, Details
    Our team can meet you at the property to discuss details, address the condition of the house, provide recommendations. Or, if you'd prefer, we can connect with the sellers directly and deal with them as an extension of your team while you focus on other priorities.
  • Preparation The Secret Sauce
    Our team will take away all the information and use their years of professional experience to build floor plans, hand pick all the furniture down to the smallest accent to be used to set-up your listing.
  • Staging (Set-Up) The Wow! Day
    Within a day, our team will transform your listing to a buyer's dream. We will set-up everything for you and have it ready for the photo-shoot.
  • Destage (Removal) You did it!
    Once you sell the listing, our team will come back to remove all our items and celebrate the success with you!

Have any Questions?



Which areas do you service?

+

We stage homes for success across the Greater Toronto Area, with occasional trips to Hamilton, Ajax and other outskirts based on the nature of the project and our partnership with the realtor.

What is the furniture rental period included in the pricing?

+

Our standard rental period is 30 days. However, we are open to customizing longer duration for certain properties and offer a flat rate for 60 – 90 days.

Where do you rent your furniture from?

+

In order to offer the best quality to our clients, we carry our own high-end collection of rental products – ranging from linen, décor to furniture. Our designers are constantly updating the collection to ensure fresh designs and a large variety. For any niche listing that require specific pieces, we have strong partnerships with some of the top furniture rental companies in Toronto. We always find a way to offer our clients the best results.
