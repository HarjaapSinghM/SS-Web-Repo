10 Reasons to Choose Studio Sheen for Your Home Staging Project in Toronto

1. Our Collection

In order to offer the best quality to our clients, we carry our own high-end collection of furniture and décor products – ranging from linen, accessories to large furniture. Our designers are constantly updating the collection to ensure fresh designs and a large variety and sources pieces from only the finest vendors internationally. We always have a way to offer our clients the best results without being limited to the collection of a rental company.

2. Our Amazing Team of Designers

Our staff is professionally trained with at least 2 to 4 years of academic experience learning the fine subtleties of design on top of their practical experience in the field. They bring a deep understanding of design styles to every project which results in work that is truly second to none.

3. Our Awards and Reviews

Since our inception, we have won several awards for our quality of work and customer service. Over the last 3 years the Real Estate Staging Association has chosen us among Top 10 Home Stagers in the country. Most importantly, our clients love us and have left amazing reviews online making us one of the highest rated and reviewed Toronto home stagers on Google.

4. Quick Turnaround Time

Real estate in Toronto moves quick, and you don’t want to spend weeks waiting for a stager while your neighbour gets the highest price on the street. With a vast inventory collection and well-trained team, we have the resources to stage even the most demanding projects within a day.

5. Customized Solutions for Each Property

Staging doesn’t always mean starting from a blank canvas. If you’re living in a house with furniture, we try our best to incorporate it into the final setup so you can save time and money in not having to move it around. In every project, we put the priorities of the customer and the buyer first.

6. No Hidden Fees

From start to finish, every step of the project is documented and at no point do our clients have to be concerned about hidden or surprise fees. We are insured as a company and that means peace of mind for our clients throughout the process.

7. We Study the Market and Understand the Buyer

No two properties are the same and nor are our stagings. A critical part of the preperation stage of each project we work on is to study the neighbourhood, learn about the demographics, and cater the design to fit the exact needs of the potential buyer accordingly. This methodology is our secret sauce.

8. We’re a One Stop Shop

Selling a home comes with a long list of tasks to complete and an orchestra of professionals to work with. When you work with us, we simplify a great deal of that work by providing a variety of trades to prepare the listing, as well as managing the project from start to finish – if you’d like.

9. Vast Experience in Providing Home Staging Services in Toronto

There isn't a single type of property we haven't yet staged in Toronto. From lofts, studios, condos, penthouses to larger homes. At this point, no home is too big or too small for our team to deliver substantial results every time.

10. Proven Results in Toronto

In a recent case, a vacant high-end condo in Yorkville sat for months in the market with 0 offers. The sellers decided to try a different approach and gave us a call to stage the property. Within days after the transformation, the property sold with 4 competing offers and $70,000 over asking. To check out some of our other recent success stories at our Why Us Page
